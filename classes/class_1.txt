Class: Employee

Attributes:
name (string)
jobTitle (string)
salaryPerHour (float)
dateHired (datetime)
responsibilities (array)
currentEmployee (boolean)

Methods:
giveRaise (Give employee more money. This method increases salaryPerHour attribute by 1.5)
fireEmployee (changes currentEmployee attribute from true to false)
addResponsibility (adds element to the end of responsibilities array)
changeTitle (makes change to jobTitle attribute)
